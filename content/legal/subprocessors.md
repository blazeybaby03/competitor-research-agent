# CompeteIQ — Subprocessors

**Last updated:** 15 June 2026
**Effective date:** 15 June 2026

This page lists the third-party service providers ("sub-processors") that CompeteIQ uses to operate the Service and that may process personal information on our behalf. It supports our [Privacy Policy](../Privacy/Privacy-Policy.md) and any Data Processing Addendum (DPA) we enter into.

> **Note:** Template prepared for CompeteIQ's records and deployment; not legal advice. Verify each provider's current hosting region and update this list when providers change.

---

## Current sub-processors

| Provider | Purpose | Data processed | Primary region |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Authentication and database hosting | Account email, name, business info, competitor URLs, reports, billing references | ap-southeast-2 (Sydney, Australia) |
| **Stripe** | Payment processing and subscription billing | Name, email, billing/payment details, subscription status | United States / global |
| **Vercel** | Application hosting and content delivery | Technical/log data, IP address, requests | United States / global edge |
| **Anthropic** | AI model that generates report content | Business and competitor inputs submitted for report generation | United States |
| **ScraperAPI** | Retrieval of public competitor web pages you nominate | Competitor URLs and retrieved public page content | United States / global |

## How we manage sub-processors

- We share only the data each provider needs to perform its function.
- We use reputable providers that offer their own security and data-protection commitments.
- Some providers process data outside Australia (see "Overseas disclosure" in our Privacy Policy).
- We review this list when we add, remove, or change a provider.

## Changes and notice

We may update our sub-processors as the Service evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date. Business customers under a Data Processing Addendum may request advance notice of new sub-processors as set out in that agreement.
