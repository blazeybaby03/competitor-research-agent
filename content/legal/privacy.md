## 1. The personal information we collect

We collect only what we need to provide the Service:

**You give us:**

- **Account details** — your email address and (optionally) your full name, created when you sign up.
- **Business information** — details about your business that you enter to generate reports (e.g. business name, website, description, industry).
- **Competitor inputs** — the competitor website URLs you submit for analysis.
- **Support communications** — the content of emails or messages you send us.

**Created when you use the Service:**

- **Reports and usage data** — the reports generated for you, your plan, report counts, and scheduling settings.
- **Billing records** — your subscription status and a Stripe customer reference. Card payments are processed by Stripe; we do **not** store your full card number.
- **Technical data** — authentication session cookies, IP address, browser/device information, and basic logs needed for security and to operate the Service.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information (such as health, racial, political, or biometric data). Please do not submit sensitive information through the Service.

## 2. How we collect it

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, enter business or competitor details, generate reports, subscribe, or contact us. Some technical data is collected automatically through cookies and server logs (see our [Cookie Policy](Cookie-Policy.md)). Billing information is collected through our payment processor, Stripe.

We use personal information to:

- create and manage your account and authenticate you;
- generate, store, and let you access competitor reports;
- process subscriptions, payments, and renewals;
- enforce plan limits and prevent abuse;
- provide support and respond to your enquiries;
- maintain the security, integrity, and performance of the Service;
- comply with our legal obligations; and
- send you service-related communications (e.g. account, billing, and security notices).

We will only use your information for a purpose listed above, a directly related purpose you would reasonably expect, or another purpose you have consented to.
