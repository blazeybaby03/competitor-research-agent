# CompeteIQ — Acceptable Use Policy

**Last updated:** 15 June 2026
**Effective date:** 15 June 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out what you may and may not do when using CompeteIQ (the "Service"). It forms part of, and is incorporated into, our [Terms of Service](Terms-of-Service.md). Capitalised terms have the meaning given in the Terms.

> **Note:** Template prepared for CompeteIQ's records and deployment; not legal advice.

---

## 1. General principle

Use the Service lawfully, responsibly, and only for legitimate competitor research in trade or commerce. Do not use it in any way that harms CompeteIQ, our suppliers, other users, or third parties.

## 2. You must not

**Break the law or infringe rights.** Do not use the Service to:

- violate any applicable law or regulation;
- infringe the intellectual property, privacy, or other rights of any person;
- access, collect, or process data you are not legally permitted to access.

**Misuse the scraping and analysis features.** Do not:

- submit URLs for websites you are prohibited from accessing, or that you know forbid automated access in a way that makes your request unlawful;
- attempt to extract personal or sensitive information about individuals;
- use the Service to harvest data for spam, surveillance, harassment, or resale as a competing data product;
- submit content protected by a paywall, login, or access control you are not authorised to bypass.

**Harm or overload systems.** Do not:

- attempt to gain unauthorised access to the Service, other accounts, or our infrastructure;
- probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service without our written permission;
- introduce malware or malicious code;
- place an unreasonable load on the Service, or interfere with its normal operation;
- circumvent plan limits, rate limits, usage caps, or access controls.

**Abuse the account model.** Do not:

- share, sell, or transfer your account or credentials;
- create multiple accounts to evade limits, free-tier caps, or a suspension;
- misrepresent your identity or authority.

**Misuse outputs.** Do not:

- present AI-generated reports as professional advice;
- use reports to defame, mislead, or unlawfully disparage a competitor;
- remove or obscure any notices in the Service.

## 3. Your responsibility for inputs

You are responsible for the URLs and information you submit and for ensuring your use complies with this AUP, applicable law, and the terms of any third-party website you nominate. CompeteIQ acts on your instructions when retrieving and analysing the content you specify.

## 4. Enforcement

We may investigate suspected breaches and, at our discretion, warn you, throttle or suspend access, remove content, or terminate your account. Serious or repeated breaches may be reported to authorities. We will act proportionately and, where practical, give you notice and an opportunity to remedy.

## 5. Reporting misuse

To report a violation of this AUP, email **support.agent@agentmail.to**.

## 6. Changes

We may update this AUP from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the Service or by email.
